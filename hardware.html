The Basics

The mouse, the monitor,and the keyboard are all pretty common things so you should know what they do, but just in case here it is.
The mouse lets you click things that are interactable , the keyboard lets you type things, and the monitor shows you what you are doing.

The Hard Drive

The hard drive is the storage of your device. It's used to keep you endless stream of selfies and mobile games you dont bother to uninstall.

The Motherboard

The Motherboard transfers data so that the other parts can work like electrical lines or the nervous system.

The RAM

RAM stands for Random Access Memory and it is what loads in what you are currently using. Wihether it is a video or a website, but after you
leave the page it disapears in to your hard drive. Kinda like how your short term memories transfer to long term.

The CPU

CPU stands for central processing unit and it controls the other parts of the computer. like how a back has an alpha male or how your brain
directs your organs.
