The Basics

Vindinium is a website where players code bots to battle other bots for control over mines in a limited amount of turns. The winner is desided by who has the most gold by the end of the 1200 turns. The player must code everything about the bot to how it moves and what playstyle you have. You can make a bot that is super aggresive or to just collect mines and hide.
